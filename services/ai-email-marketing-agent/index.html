AI Email Marketing Agent

Cold outreach that feels like a warm handshake.

The Hyper-Personalizer

It visits each lead's LinkedIn and company site. Instead of 'Dear Sir', it writes: 'Saw you just launched Feature X—here is how we can help scale it.' Response rates triple.

The Intelligent Follow-Up

It doesn't just spam. If a lead replies 'Ask me in Q3', the agent understands the date, pauses the sequence, and automatically wakes up on July 1st to reconnect.

The Meeting Booker

When a lead shows interest, the agent handles the back-and-forth scheduling: 'Does Tuesday at 2 PM work?' and sends the calendar invite automatically.
